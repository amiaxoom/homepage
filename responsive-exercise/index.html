Kuchipatchi has a round, green, jelly bean-like body with two large lips, beady eyes, stubby arms and legs, and light pea-green cheeks (which were added in the Connection era). On the Tamagotchi L.i.f.e. app, he can also be pink or yellow. Kuchipatchi has a carefree and gentle nature. He is mild and lazy, but very romantic and a daydreamer. He sometimes cheats if playing games. He has a supersized passion for food, especially Tama-Donuts, and he also loves to take naps. He also loves relaxing in hot springs and will sometimes sleep in one.
